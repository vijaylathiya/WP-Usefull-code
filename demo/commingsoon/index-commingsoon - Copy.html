The Problem:

Health Care Supply Chain that relied on "as ready" production models for essential products, which was capable of handling demands dictated by normal times. This fragile, antiquated, invisible supply chain for multi-billion health care providers was a catastrophic failure. It collapsed under the load. We have learned a very sobering lesson from COVID-19, just how vulnerable and fractured the Health Care supply chain infrastructure in our country. This system puts workers, patients, and the general public at risk for the shortage and inferior quality of Personal Protection Equipment (PPE) available from the 3rd world.

Our Solution:

The solution to the problem is creating a point to point fully visible supply chain of Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) here at home that puts the health care service providers and the general public first.
